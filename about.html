FAQ

Answers to popular questions

How long does the installation of doors/windows take?

The duration of the installation process depends on various factors such as the size and complexity of the project. On average, the installation of doors/windows typically takes between one to three days. However, for more extensive projects or custom-designed installations, it may require additional time.

What types of materials are used for door/window installation?

An array of materials is utilized for door/window installation, encompassing aluminum, vinyl, wood, and fiberglass. These materials are selected based on various factors including durability, energy efficiency, aesthetics, and budget considerations.

Do you offer warranty for your installation services?

Yes, we provide a warranty for our installation services. Our warranty coverage ensures peace of mind for our customers, guaranteeing the quality and durability of our workmanship.

Can I customize the design of my doors/windows?

Certainly! We offer customization options for the design of your doors and windows. Whether you have specific preferences in terms of style, size, color, or functionality, our team will work closely with you to create tailored solutions that perfectly match your vision and requirements.

Is there any additional cost for removal of old doors/windows during installation?

There might be an additional cost associated with the removal of old doors and windows during the installation process. Factors such as the size, type, and condition of the existing doors and windows can influence the extent of the work required for their removal. We can provide you with a detailed breakdown of any additional costs involved based on the specifics of your project.
